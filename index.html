Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

HelloBoe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, and especially the privacy of children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use HelloBoe, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and authentication data (such as Apple ID or Google account) when you create an account.
  • Child profile data: Information you provide about your child, including age range and responses to our temperament assessment questionnaire.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used and session duration.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support purposes.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

HelloBoe is designed for parents and caregivers of children aged 4 months to 36 months. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All data is collected from parents or legal guardians. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable international children's privacy laws.

Parents may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's data at any time by contacting us at hello@helloboe.com.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the HelloBoe app experience
  • To generate temperament profiles and personalised activity recommendations
  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our services through aggregated, anonymised analytics
  • To comply with our legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or your child's data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform, strictly under confidentiality agreements and only as necessary to deliver our services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, strict access controls, and regular security reviews to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@helloboe.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of HelloBoe after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
